About Project

LOOW application is built for Volunteers, Orphanage, and Restaurants to utilize our app to make an opportunity to transfer their excess food to orphanage.

  • Orphanage : Orphanage application are the user who is going to use the app to get leftover food.

  • Restaurants : Restaurants are the one who are going to add the food in the app.
  • Volunteer : Volunteers will deliver the leftover food from restaurant to orpahange.

  • Donation : People can donate funds to loow and it will be transfered to orphanage.

  • Github: Application hosted in github.
